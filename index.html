Ben “The Creative Coder”

Student • Web Developer • Idea Maker

Hey there! I’m Ben, a 13-year-old student who built Laugh Lounge after plenty of late-night coding sessions (and maybe a few “I’ll do homework later” moments). I love bringing people joy — whether it’s through a funny meme, a clever feature, or a hidden game that sneaks past boring moments.

This site was a simple school project, but it turned into something much bigger. I wanted a fun, chill place online where anyone can laugh, play, and just relax — no ads, no trackers, no nonsense.

Fun Fact: I once finished an entire coding project in less than a day. Full focus, zero distractions.
