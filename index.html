Hey there! I'm Max, a 17-year-old high school senior who built Laugh Lounge during late-night coding sessions (and a few skipped study halls). I love making people smile — whether it's through a perfectly timed meme, a hilarious video, or a secret stash of unblocked games when the school Wi-Fi says "no."
